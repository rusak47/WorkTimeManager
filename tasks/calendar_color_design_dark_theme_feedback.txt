# Calendar accessibility description — May 2026

## Overall layout

A dark-themed monthly calendar widget. The outer container is a rounded rectangle with background color `#1e2535` (dark navy blue). Navigation arrows sit left and right of the month title. The title reads **May 2026** in light text `#e2e6f0` at approximately 20px.

> **Note on title visibility:** The heading is rendered in `#e2e6f0` against `#1e2535` — contrast ratio is approximately 8.5:1, which passes WCAG AA/AAA. However the title blends visually with the dark chrome and is not reinforced by any border, background highlight, or size contrast beyond 20px. A sighted user might scan past it. For a blind agent, the text content itself is unambiguous; the visual problem is purely aesthetic (low perceived hierarchy on screen).

---

## Column headers (row 1)

Seven columns, Monday through Sunday, left to right.

Labels: **Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun**

Text color `#7a8299` (muted blue-gray), 12px, all-caps weight.

---

## Day cell types — color reference

Each cell is a small rounded rectangle (8px radius). Five visual states:

| Type | Background | Border | Number color | Meaning |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Workday | `#252e45` dark blue-gray | transparent | `#c8d0e8` light lavender | Normal working day |
| Weekend | `#1d2438` darker navy | transparent | `#8090b0` muted slate | Sat / Sun |
| Holiday | `#0d3d35` deep teal-black | `#1a6b5e` teal, 1.5px | `#4ecfb5` bright mint | Full holiday |
| Memorial | `#2a2050` deep violet-black | `#5046a0` violet, 1.5px | `#a899f5` soft lavender-purple | Memorial day type |
| Short day | `#3a2d10` dark amber-brown | `#7a5a1a` warm gold, 1.5px | `#f0b840` bright amber | Reduced hours day |
| Out-of-month | Same cell structure, `opacity: 0.35` | — | `#7a8299` | Days from April / June |

### Tracked session dot

A small filled circle `5×5px`, color `#5b8df5` (medium cornflower blue), centered horizontally, pinned to the bottom of the cell. Present on most workdays and some special days to indicate a session was logged.
